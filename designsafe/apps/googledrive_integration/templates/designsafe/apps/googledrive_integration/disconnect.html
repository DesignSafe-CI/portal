Disconnect Google Drive

By disconnecting your Google Drive account from DesignSafe, you will no longer be able to browse files from Google Drive on DesignSafe. This will not delete any files from either DesignSafe or Google Drive. What will happen is that you will be removing the authorization you granted DesignSafe to manage files and folders in your Google Drive account. For further information on usage, see the Privacy Policy.

To reiterate, this will not delete any files. If you wish to delete the files from either your Google Drive storage or your DesignSafe storage you will need to do that manually.

If you would like to disconnect your Google Drive account from DesignSafe, you can do so by clicking the button below.
