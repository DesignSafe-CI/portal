Register an Account

Congratulations! Your account registration has been received.

Before you can log in to DesignSafe-CI you will need to complete your account activation. Follow the steps below to activate your account:

  1. You will receive an email with a link to confirm your email. Check your spam folder if you do not see it. This does NOT give you access to the portal at this stage.
  2. Once your registration has been approved (typically within 1 business day), you will receive an acceptance email at the address you provided.
  3. Follow the link in the email to activate your account.
  4. Provide the username and password you chose at registration to activate your account.

That's it! Thank you for registering for DesignSafe-CI. If you have any additional questions or if you are unable to complete your account activation, please submit a Help Desk ticket.

Please do not request a password reset before you have followed all the steps outlined above. Doing so can cause problems with making sure your account becomes fully activated.
