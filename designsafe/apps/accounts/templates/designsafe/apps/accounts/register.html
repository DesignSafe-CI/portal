Register an Account

If you already have a TACC account, simply log in with your TACC username and password to access DesignSafe.

If you do not have an account, please use the TACC User Registration form to request an account. Once your account has been approved, you will then be able to log into DesignSafe.

For citizens of countries designated as “countries of concern” by United States government regulations, additional documentation in the form of a currently valid visa and passport will be requested after the account registration form has been submitted. Citizens of these countries are only eligible to have accounts while legally residing in the United States.
